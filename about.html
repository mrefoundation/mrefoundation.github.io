About Us

Mathematical Research-Education Foundation is a private research organization focusing in applied mathematics: infectious disease modelling, applications of nonlinear partial differential equations (typically in water wave modelling), and neural network applications (for infectious disease inference and software products).

Currently, our non-profit activity includes: 1. Education of Mathematical Olympiad to young Indonesian students (with minimum or no experience in math olympiad) to increase competitiveness and encourage achievements.
