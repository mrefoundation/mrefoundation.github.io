Mathematics Research and Education Foundation

MREF is a private organization in Indonesia that aims to produce both mathematical research and educational services.

Online Tutoring Sessions:
We provide online tutoring sessions and more. Mathematics is a language. Like learning any language, at first it feels unnatural, but as we make it a habit it will become visceral. Therefore, we encourage students to not only attend classes, but to work on many problems, beginning from the simplest ones to the intricate ones. Every student can be fluent in math by first strengthening the basics and fundamentals before gradually mastering further topics.

Research Scope

Mathematical modelling, infectious disease modelling, numerical solution of PDE, Fourier spectral method, and neural network.
